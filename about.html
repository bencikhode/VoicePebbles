About VoicePebbles

At VoicePebbles, we’re dedicated to revolutionizing speech therapy with innovative, zero-prep resources designed by experts for experts. Our mission is to empower VoicePebbleses with a seamless platform that delivers engaging tools for every therapy goal.

VoicePebbles was founded to simplify the VoicePebbles experience, offering over 40,000 real-life images, interactive games, and activities that require no preparation. Our web-based solutions are crafted for teletherapy and in-person sessions, ensuring every student—from preschool to high school—can learn, express, and grow with confidence.

Meet Our Founder

Sumeet Khode is the visionary behind VoicePebbles, bringing a unique blend of education and leadership to the field of speech therapy. Educated at Lockheed Martin Adam Smith University in the USA, Sumeet hails from Cranbourne West, Victoria, Australia, where his passion for accessible learning took root. With a strong command of English, he identified a critical need for innovative, ready-to-use materials during his exploration of educational technologies.

Driven by a commitment to empower VoicePebbleses, Sumeet founded VoicePebbles to create a comprehensive library of resources, including interactive online games and over 10,000 real-life images. His vision is to eliminate the time-consuming task of material preparation, allowing therapists to focus on student progress. With a network of 1,000 followers and 500+ professional connections, Sumeet continues to inspire a global community of educators and therapists through his innovative approach.

How VoicePebbles Works

Getting started is simple! Just enter your therapy target into the search bar of our materials library, and instantly access hundreds of games, activities, and resources—zero prep required. Our web-based tools are designed for screen-sharing in teletherapy, with interactive features that let students participate directly.

FAQs

  • How does the subscription work? Can I cancel at any time?

    We offer a 2-week free trial. After that, our subscription is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime online by visiting the “My Account” tab, navigating to “Subscriptions,” and clicking “Cancel.”

  • How can I use your materials in my sessions?

    Our web-based games and activities are perfect for screen-sharing during teletherapy. Most platforms allow keyboard/mouse control sharing, enabling student interaction.

  • Is a subscription for one user?

    Yes, each subscription is for one VoicePebbles user.

  • Do you offer group accounts for districts/private practices/universities?

    Yes, we provide bulk subscriptions with discounts based on the number of users. Please email us for a quote.

  • Do you have an annual subscription?

    Yes, annual plans are available—see our Pricing page for details and savings!

  • Do you take requests for certain materials?

    Absolutely! We welcome your suggestions and add them to our to-do list, addressing them as soon as possible.

  • How do I use the Save Lists feature?

    Our Save Lists feature lets you create and title as many lists as you’d like, saving materials for easy access. Organize them on the “Saved Lists” page.
